Welcome to my homepage!

I am an Associate Professor and Research Fellow, with extensive experience in agricultural economics, environmental sustainability, and food systems.

My current research endeavors revolve around the application of behavioral economic theories and experimental economic methods. My primary focus lies in unraveling the intricate connections between food systems, food policy, and human decision-making processes that underpin agricultural sustainability concerns.

My research covers both consumption and production sides of agricultural systems. My work has been published in peer-reviewed journals such as Agribusiness: An International Journal, Applied Economics, Ecological Economics, HortTechnology, and Water Resources Research.

  • Agricultural Economics and Food Policy
