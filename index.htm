Welcome to my homepage!

I am an Associate Professor at Huanggang Normal University and a researcher specializing in agricultural economics and environmental sustainability. Currently, I also serve as a Research Fellow at Metharath University (Thailand) and INTI International University (Malaysia), alongside my role as a Consultant/Resource Person for the Asian Development Bank. As an Executive Member of the Cross-Border E-commerce Research & Practice Group at Huazhong Agricultural University, I contribute to advancing research in agricultural economics and sustainability.

My research work has been published in high-impact journals with a cumulative impact factor exceeding 170, including Science of the Total Environment (IF: 9.80), Environmental Science and Pollution Research (IF: 5.80), and Frontiers in Environmental Science. I have made significant contributions to understanding groundwater markets, agricultural sustainability, and food systems.
