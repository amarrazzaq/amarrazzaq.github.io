Outreach Work

Innovation & Enterprise

As a founding member of the E-commerce Research Group at Huazhong Agricultural University (HZAU), I contributed to pioneering initiatives in cross-border e-commerce. Our team successfully organized 7 consecutive international forums across China, Bangladesh, and Pakistan, focusing on empowering students and entrepreneurs in the digital marketplace.

International Collaboration

Led the establishment of the Sino-Pak Cross-Border E-commerce Association, working with HZAU researchers to implement the successful Taobao Village model in Pakistan. This initiative aims to revolutionize rural e-commerce and enhance local communities' economic opportunities.

Community Impact

Successfully trained numerous students from invarious countries in cross-border e-commerce practices, particularly focusing on rural e-commerce. In addition, successfully organized the International Cross-Border E-commerce Summer School in Ili (Xinjiang, China) in 2024. Many success stories and collaborations came out of this initiative

Rural Finance Initiative

Serving as Manager of YOU Microfinance Fund, a pioneering initiative led by Prof Zhou Deyi, I oversee the provision of interest-free microfinance loans to farmers across Kenya, Pakistan, and Bangladesh. This program empowers rural communities by providing essential financial support for agricultural development and sustainable farming practices.
