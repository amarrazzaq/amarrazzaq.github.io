Welcome

I am an Associate Professor and researcher specializing in the economic and environmental dimensions of modern agriculture. My work is focused on creating resilient food systems, addressing challenges in water management, and informing effective food policy.

With affiliations at Huanggang Normal University and as a consultant for the Asian Development Bank, my research contributes to a deeper understanding of sustainable agricultural practices from both a production and consumption perspective. My findings have been published in leading international journals, including Science of the Total Environment and the Canadian Journal of Agricultural Economics.
