Amar Razzaq, Associate Professor of Agricultural Economics

Welcome to My Academic Portfolio

I am a researcher specializing in the economic and environmental dimensions of modern agriculture. My work focuses on building resilient food systems, advancing water management strategies, and developing evidence for effective food policy.

With affiliations at Huanggang Normal University and as a consultant for the Asian Development Bank, my research contributes to sustainable agricultural practices from production and consumption perspectives. My work has appeared in Science of the Total Environment, Journal of Retailing and Consumer Services, and the Canadian Journal of Agricultural Economics.
